While all our multi-day tours require riders of experience, some are more demanding than others:

Intermediate Tours: Suitable for riders who ride regularly and are comfortable riding and controlling a horse at walk, rising / posting trot, and canter.

Advanced Tours: Suitable for riders competent and confident at riding at walk, rising / posting trot, sitting trot, and canter / gallop. Can saddle up a horse, adjust girth and stirrups unaided.
