This coming summer we will offer a large variety of multi-day riding tours all around Iceland. While the tours vary in terms of length and level of difficulty, they will all allow you to discover the Icelandic horse in Iceland’s vast and spectacular nature and hold promise for an unforgettable experience. In most cases we will be travelling with a herd of free running horses. This allows for us to change horses several times during day, for our pleasure and the health of the horses, and we take great pride in our commitment of Smaller Groups, Bigger Herds.

Below, you will find an overview of all our tours, their level of difficulty and scheduled dates. While some departures are already sold out, we still have a few seats available on others. By ticking the ‘Tours with Available Seats’, you will find the tours that are still open for booking. Please note that the number of seats is very limited, so we urge you to secure your seat as soon as possible.

Please do not hesitate to contact us should you have questions or need further information.

Intermediate: A person who rides regularly. Is competent at riding and control a horse at walk, rising / posting trot, and canter.

Advanced: A person who is competent and confident at riding a horse at walk, rising / posting trot, sitting trot, and canter / gallop. Can saddle up a horse, adjust girth and stirrups unaided.
